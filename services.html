What we do

Maximized works across two complementary practices: dependable IT and infrastructure consulting, and agent-first software development.

IT & Infrastructure Consulting Agent-First Software Development

Practice 01

IT & Infrastructure Consulting

Design, automation, and hardening for the systems your organization depends on — on-premises and in the cloud.

  • Virtualization & server infrastructure

    Design and management of Proxmox clusters and Windows Server estates, including high availability, storage, backups, and disaster recovery.

  • Cloud & Microsoft 365 administration

    Azure and Microsoft 365 tenant administration — Entra ID, Intune, Exchange Online, conditional access, and secure baseline configuration.

  • Automation & scripting

    PowerShell modules, scheduled jobs, and operational tooling that standardize tasks, reduce errors, and free up your team's time.

  • Security hardening & identity

    Identity and access management, network segmentation, and defense-in-depth reviews to reduce risk across hybrid environments.

  • PKI & certificate management

    Certificate authority design, certificate lifecycle automation, and trust-chain hardening to keep encrypted services dependable.

  • Documentation & process

    Clear runbooks, architecture documentation, and repeatable processes so environments stay maintainable as teams and needs change.

Practice 02

Agent-First Software Development

We build software with AI agents as first-class collaborators — from agentic workflows to LLM-powered product features — shipped with an AI-assisted delivery process.

  • Agentic application development

    Custom applications and services designed around autonomous and human-in-the-loop agents that take real work off your team's plate.

  • LLM & AI integration

    Adding large language model features to existing products — retrieval, summarization, classification, and natural-language interfaces.

  • AI-assisted delivery

    An agent-first engineering workflow that ships well-tested, documented code faster, without cutting corners on quality.

  • Custom tooling & automation pipelines

    Internal tools, integrations, and automation pipelines — including agent tooling and MCP servers — tailored to how your team works.

  • Technical discovery & architecture

    Pragmatic discovery, scoping, and architecture so an AI-driven build solves the right problem and fits your existing systems.

  • Secure & responsible AI

    Guardrails, data handling, and access controls so AI features are deployed safely and stay aligned with your security posture.
