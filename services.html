Services

What we do

Maximized helps organizations stabilize infrastructure, reduce operational toil, and build practical software with modern AI capabilities. Engagements are scoped around real delivery: assessment, implementation, documentation, and handoff.

IT & Infrastructure Consulting Agent-First Software Development

Practice 01

IT & Infrastructure Consulting

Design, automation, and hardening for the systems your organization depends on — on-premises and in the cloud.

  • Windows & identity administration

    Active Directory, Group Policy, DNS/DHCP, Entra ID, hybrid identity, and access-control cleanup.

  • Virtualization & platform operations

    Proxmox, Hyper-V, storage, backups, clustering, and recovery planning for resilient platforms.

  • Microsoft 365 & cloud administration

    Tenant hardening, policy baselines, Exchange Online, Intune, and Conditional Access.

  • Automation & operational tooling

    PowerShell and Python workflows that reduce repetitive work and improve consistency.

  • PKI & trust services

    Certificate authority design, certificate lifecycle management, and service reliability improvements.

  • Documentation & runbooks

    Architecture, standards, and procedures that make environments maintainable as teams change.

Ideal when

  • You're between IT hires and need senior coverage without a full-time role.
  • An environment needs cleanup, migration, or hardening before it grows further.
  • Manual, undocumented processes are slowing your team down.

Practice 02

Agent-First Software Development

Practical software that uses AI where it genuinely helps — built with maintainability, guardrails, and real business use in mind.

  • Internal tools with AI-assisted workflows

    Applications that help teams retrieve information, classify data, summarize activity, and automate repetitive processes.

  • LLM feature integration

    Natural-language interfaces, search and retrieval, summarization, and controlled AI assistance inside existing workflows.

  • Custom agent tooling

    MCP-compatible tooling, workflow orchestration, and human-in-the-loop systems designed with access controls and auditability in mind.

  • Technical discovery for AI projects

    Scoping where AI actually helps, where it does not, and how to deploy it safely.

Ideal when

  • You have a repetitive, knowledge-heavy workflow worth streamlining.
  • You want to add AI features to an existing product without compromising security.
  • You're unsure where AI fits and need honest, practical scoping.

Security and documentation, as standard

Every engagement includes security-conscious defaults and clear documentation as standard deliverables — not optional add-ons.
