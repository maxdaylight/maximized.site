Privacy Policy

Your privacy

This policy explains what information Maximized collects through this website and how it is used. Last updated June 19, 2026.

The short version

Maximized does not use advertising or analytics tracking, and this site does not set cookies. The only information we collect is what you choose to send us through the contact form or by email, and we use it only to respond to your inquiry.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form, we receive the details you provide — typically your name, email address, an optional subject, and your message. We do not require any information beyond what is needed to respond. We do not collect data from visitors who simply browse the site.

How the contact form works

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form-handling service. When you submit the form, your information is transmitted to Formspree, which forwards it to us by email. Formspree's handling of your data is governed by its own privacy policy. If you prefer not to use the form, you can email us directly instead.

How we use your information

Information you submit is used only to review your inquiry, respond to your request, and, where relevant, discuss a potential engagement. We do not sell or rent your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not use cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics. Your theme preference (light or dark) is applied in memory for the current visit only and is not stored or shared.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. As with most web hosts, GitHub may process basic technical request information (such as IP address and browser type) for security and operational purposes, as described in GitHub's privacy documentation.

Data retention

Messages you send are retained only as long as needed to respond to and follow up on your inquiry. You can ask us to delete your message and contact details at any time.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you have sent us. To make a request, or if you have any questions about this policy, contact us at maxdaylight@maximized.site.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices or the services we use change. Material updates will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
