Background

How I approach the work

My career centers on the unglamorous-but-critical layer of technology: the servers, networks, identities, and automation that keep organizations operating. I've built and maintained Windows Server and Active Directory environments, run virtualization clusters on Proxmox, and administered Microsoft cloud tenants across Azure and Microsoft 365.

I believe good infrastructure is deliberate. I document thoroughly, automate repeatable tasks with PowerShell, and design with security and recoverability in mind from day one. The goal is always the same: systems that are stable, understandable, and safe to operate.

When I'm not in the terminal, I'm sharpening my toolkit — writing reusable scripts, exploring new platforms, and refining the processes that make complex environments easier to run.
