About Maximized

Dependable operations, secure architecture, pragmatic automation

Maximized is led by a systems engineer with a background spanning managed-services and high-scale enterprise environments — well over a hundred client networks and thousands of users and devices. The focus is dependable operations: incident response, root-cause analysis, and automation that removes repetitive work, paired with the documentation and process discipline that keep systems maintainable long after handoff.

Founder & Principal Engineer

Max Daylight

Across managed-services providers and high-scale enterprise support, the work has centered on environments where uptime, security, and fast resolution are non-negotiable — more than a hundred client environments and thousands of users and devices. The throughline is turning recurring problems into permanent fixes through incident response, root-cause analysis, and disciplined documentation.

That operational background shapes every engagement. Repetitive administration is automated with PowerShell, Python, and shell scripting; recurring issues are designed out through deliberate process redesign; and measurable improvements — lower ticket volume, reduced ticket age, faster response, and quicker onboarding — come from steady refinement rather than heroics. Technical breadth spans Microsoft 365, Azure, and Entra ID / Active Directory, virtualization and disaster recovery, networking, and security tooling, with working familiarity across AWS, GCP, VMware, and Citrix — plus self-hosted, private AI that keeps sensitive data on your own infrastructure. Clients work directly with the engineer doing the work.
