About Maximized

Infrastructure you can rely on, software built for what's next

Maximized is an IT consulting and software development company based in Scottsdale, Arizona. We're owned and primarily engineered by Max Daylight, and we work on-site locally and remotely for clients everywhere.

The company

Why Maximized exists

Maximized was built on a simple idea: the infrastructure and software that organizations run on should be dependable, well-documented, and genuinely understood by the people maintaining it. We bring that discipline to two practices — IT and infrastructure consulting, and agent-first software development.

As a focused, owner-operated company, you work directly with the engineer doing the work. That means clear communication, accountable delivery, and solutions designed for your environment rather than a one-size-fits-all template.

Owner & primary engineer

Max Daylight

Max is the owner and primary engineer of Maximized. His career centers on the critical layer of technology: the servers, networks, identities, and automation that keep organizations operating. He has built and maintained Windows Server and Active Directory environments, run virtualization clusters on Proxmox, and administered Microsoft cloud tenants across Azure and Microsoft 365.

Today that infrastructure foundation is paired with an agent-first approach to building software — using AI agents as first-class collaborators to design, build, test, and document custom tools and applications. The goal is always the same: systems that are stable, understandable, and safe to operate.
